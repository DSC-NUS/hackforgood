Problem Statements

DSC NUS presents Hack For Good, a student-run weekend-long hackathon which seeks to inspire students to learn and use technology for social causes. Our innovation challenge is unique in that we are posing real problem statements provided by NPOs in Singapore. Participating teams are expected to create technological prototypes as part of their solution to tackle their chosen statement and directly benefit the NPO backing each statement.

About the Hackathon

Eligibility + Registeration

  • This hackathon is open to all students from tertiary educational institutions — Polytechnics, Universities, ITEs
  • Applications are of first-come-first-serve basis.
  • Participation is free of charge!

Team Forming

  • A team must have 4 to 6 members. You do not have to be from the same school
  • Uf you are unable to form a team, you can use our team-forming Slack channel to seek out like-minded individuals.

Projects

  • You can use any programming language, framework or technology to build your idea
  • The challenge statements will be released 1 week in advance and you are encouraged to start brainstorming and prototyping before Day 1
  • Use the actual hackathon day to demonstrate your prototypes to our partners and get feedback on how to refine your idea.
  • Copying of existing ideas or recycling of entire projects will not be allowed.
  • Your team will own all intellectual property rights to your work.
